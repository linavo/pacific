About Our Goal

Pacific Trails Resort is offer to you by a team of wilderness lovers, and enjoy bringing others to share our common goal, relaxation. As you glimpse into the shimmering ocean, you can feel the warm, grainy sand touch the tips of your toes.

The beautiful gaze of the Pacific Coast is not only limited during the daytime, at night, you will be able to enjoy the beautiful night sky as the stars light up and the world relaxes. Pacific Trails Resort is dedicated to providing our customers with an experience they've never experienced before. We dedicate ourselves as a team to provide countless things to do such as hiking, kayaking, and bird watching. As we take pride in our joy of having our customers leave wanting more, we will be providing additional information when new modifications are made.
